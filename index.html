Turn Your AI Cameras Into a Data Source

AI Data Collect is a self-hosted platform that captures the structured metadata your AI-capable IP cameras already produce — events, occupancy, dwell, heatmaps — and stores it on your hardware. No video on the wire. No cloud. No GPU.

Ingest

Store

Persists every event to a secure on-premise time-series database with configurable 30/60/90-day retention. Years of analytics in minimal space.

Surface

About the Platform

A Data Layer for Your AI Camera Fleet

Modern IP cameras run AI onboard and produce a continuous stream of structured events. AI Data Collect captures that stream, persists it, and makes it usable — all on hardware you own, in environments with zero outbound internet.

  • Self-hosted on your hardware — no cloud, no SaaS
  • Air-gap ready — designed for zero outbound internet
  • Metadata only — no video on the wire, no GPU required
  • NDAA & TAA compliance posture built in

Security by Architecture

Internal certificate authority, TLS everywhere, encrypted credentials at rest, two-factor authentication, and role-based access control with per-site scoping across every endpoint.

One-Shot Install

A single install script with port pre-flight, automatic shift on collision, post-install shortcuts, encrypted access codes, and an end-to-end first-run setup wizard. Deployable in restricted environments.

Unified Retention

30/60/90-day configurable retention with automatic heatmap and snapshot purge. Retention is a deployment knob, not a storage procurement conversation.
